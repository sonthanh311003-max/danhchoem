# MemoryOS Signature Opening Experience Specification
# Version 1.0

The opening sequence is not a loading screen, nor is it a splash screen. It is a ritual. It prepares the user emotionally to transition from the logical digital world into a sacred space of memories.

---

## ⏳ The Opening Timeline (Total: 8–12 seconds)

### Scene 1: Silence & Atmosphere (0s - 2s)
* Screen is almost dark warm paper texture with subtle film grain.
* Tiny floating dust particles.
* Ambient room soundscape (wind, distant birds).
* No UI elements.

### Scene 2: The Vessel (2s - 4s)
* Soft golden sunlight fades in at the center.
* Reveals a handcrafted cotton envelope with a wax seal resting in the center.
* Deep paper shadows create tactile depth.

### Scene 3: Tactile Intimacy (4s - 6s)
* Hovering over the envelope triggers subtle rotate/tilt physics.
* The wax seal catches the light, inviting interaction without explicit instructions.

### Scene 4: The Unfolding (Triggered on Click)
* 200ms physical delay.
* Wax seal breaks, envelope opens, and letter slides upward with realistic paper unfolding motion.

### Scene 5: Cinematic Message (6s - 10s)
* Camera zooms closer.
* Soft piano melody fades in.
* Headlines fade in sequentially:
  1. *"Some stories deserve more than a photograph."*
  2. *(Pause)* *"They deserve a place to live forever."*

### Scene 6: Transition to Action (10s - 12s)
* The paper texture transforms into the first page of a memory book.
* Main CTA "Begin Your Story" and secondary "Continue where I left off" fade in gracefully.
