---

# PURPOSE

A Digital Memories experience is not a website. It is an emotional journey.
Every transition should explain the shift in attention and story progression.

---

# MOTION RULES

* **Natural Physics**: Motion should explain, never decorate. Prefer Framer Motion spring physics.
* **Camera Movement**: Keep the central memory object stable; gently move, zoom, or blur the camera to guide attention.
* **Pacing**: Alternate between fast-paced scenes (Polaroid gallery, timelines) and slow cinematic scenes (opening envelope, reading letters).
* **Cinematic Transitions**: Use depth blurs, fades, and realistic page flips instead of raw slide-in animations.
