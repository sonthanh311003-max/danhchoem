# PURPOSE

Components are emotional keepsake experiences.
Every component exists to strengthen a specific memory.

---

# COMPONENT EXPERIENCE STANDARD

* **IntroLetter**: Centered, realistic 3D envelope with perspective, Wax Seal crack sequence, handwriting readable sheets. Keyboard support: Enter/Space to open, Escape to fold.
* **Book**: Curvature shadows, realistic page dimensions, touch and keyboard page-flipping (`ArrowLeft` / `ArrowRight`).
* **Locket**: Reveal hidden treasure memories with a heavy weighted physics transition.
* **MusicPlayer**: Warm ambience, subtle volume fades, optional playback, no aggressive dark UI.
* **Timeline**: Milestones feel important, allow expanding stories, location pin maps, and ambient cues.
